I am from El Barrio, also known as Spanish Harlem and also known as East Harlem. My neighborhood has gone through many changes over the last 30 years, but one thing remains and it's the creativity of its residents. One of my favorite things about El Barrio is the folkloric and street art on our buildings. Above are examples of some well know artists whose artwork celebrates the culture and history of our neighborhood and our people. The image on the left is "Ode in East Harlem" by Manny Vega. The image on the right is a mural of the legendary singer Celia Cruz by James De La Vega.
