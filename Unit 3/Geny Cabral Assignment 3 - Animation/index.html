Meet Frida

Like my namesake, I am many things to many people; Artist, traveler, writer, lover, sister, mother and muse. My passions include living my life to the fullest and meeting other disrupters, vagabonds and curious minds. I hail from Oakland, CA but spend most of my time on the road.
